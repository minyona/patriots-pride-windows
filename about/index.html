Four Generations

Four generations
of craftsmanship.

The family in this photograph built things to last — by hand, with pride, generations before any of us picked up the trade. That heritage runs straight through Patriots' Pride today.

We didn't start as a contractor. We started as a consulting firm, brought in to teach companies up and down the East Coast how to install windows the right way. Time and again, we saw what happens when a company cares more about closing the sale than the family left living with the result.

So we built the company we wished those families had called first — veteran-owned, owner-operated, and run on a single promise.

"No homeowner left behind."

The company story

That standard is the whole company. As a veteran-owned, 4th-generation installer, we set out to deliver not just the product we sell, but the solution that fits your home, your needs and your budget — and to stand behind it long after the crew has packed up and gone.

Our experience is drawn from not just what works well — but from seeing, over and over, what doesn't. That's why we provide signed timeline agreements between both parties: executable timelines for communication and delivery, so we stay accountable and accurate.

Our consultative approach erases the lines of the old sales box, where customer and crew work in tandem to define the right solution. 15,000+ happy customers up and down the East Coast and growing — your comfort and safety guaranteed by the Patriot's Promise.

Don't get left behind working with a company that only cares about getting your money.

Meet the owners

Judson Simpson & Rutger Conradi are two owner-operators with 20+ years of experience apiece and four generations of window-installation craft behind them. Both front-end and backend operations are owner-covered, so nothing falls through the cracks. They're a veteran-owned business and proud of it — which is exactly why the Hometown Hero program gives 50% off exterior doors to those who serve.

What makes us different

Most companies in this trade are sales-and-marketing organizations. They bring one window to your house, run a 10-step selling system, stay two-plus hours, and won't install a window you bought somewhere else. We do the opposite — because we're a true 4th-generation install company, manufacturer-direct on every product, and we'll happily install owner-supplied product to spec.
