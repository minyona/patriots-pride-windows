Our Story

The replacement window company that doesn't act like one.

Patriots' Pride started as a consulting firm — training installers up and down the East Coast on how to put a window in the wall correctly. Most companies on this list of three trades bring one window into your kitchen, run a ten-step pitch, and stay for two hours.

That isn't us. We're owner-operators with 20+ years on the truck — front-end and backend covered, nothing falling through the cracks. We work manufacturer-direct on every brand we install, and yes — if you bought your windows somewhere else, we'll still put them in.

Judson Simpson & Rutger Conradi
