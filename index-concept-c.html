Our Story

Started training installers. Now we are the installers.

Patriots' Pride began as a consulting firm — training installers up and down the East Coast on how to put a window in the wall correctly. The market needed someone who knew what wasn't working before they sold what was.

Today, we're owner-operators with 20+ years on the truck, manufacturer-direct on every brand we install, and we cover the entire SE TN tri-state market plus Atlanta and Northeast Alabama. The Patriot's Promise — signed timelines, lifetime warranties, owner accountability — is non-negotiable on every job we run.
