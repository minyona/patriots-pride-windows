What the promise means

The Patriot's Promise isn't marketing language — it's the operating standard we built the company around after years of seeing what other contractors do wrong. Every Patriots' Pride job includes a signed timeline agreement between both parties, so communication and delivery are documented and accountable from day one.

Your installation is backed by a lifetime material-and-labor warranty on windows (fully transferable one time, then prorated), 30 years on siding, and 15 years on entry doors. We carry $2M in coverage and workers' comp on every job, and the owners stay personally accountable from estimate through final walk-through.

Hometown Hero program

As a veteran-owned company, we take 50% off any exterior door for active military, veterans, police, fire, EMS and dispatchers. Just mention HERO at your free estimate.
